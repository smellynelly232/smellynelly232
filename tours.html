About Us

Hour long walking tour covering multiple homes in the northeast part of downtown Raleigh, NC.
Meet at the North Carolina State Capital Building at 1 E Morgan St.
Meet on the side with the statue dedicated to North Carolinian presidents.
Tour starts at 7:15. Plan to arrive 15-30 mins early. Tour ends very close to the meeting point.

FAQ

What should I bring on the tour?

Comfortable walking shoes, water, and weather-appropriate clothing.

How long does the tour last?

Our walking tours typically last between 1 to 1.5 hours.

Are pets allowed?

Pets are only allowed on private tours. However, we do allow service animals.

What happens if it rains?

We operate rain or shine, so please dress accordingly. Tours will be cancelled if weather is too dangerous

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes. We do not do last minute bookings.

Are there any restrooms along the way?

There will not be any restrooms along the way, it is mostly residential. Many business downtown will only let you use the restoom if you buy something.

Would my kids enjoy this?

This tour is rich in history and architecture which is great for adults who love story telling, but some children may not be as engaged the entire time

Where can we park?

There is a lot of decks, lots, and street parking on every side of the capital building as well as a parking lot inbetween the Christ Church and the Highway Building
